My Cause

Gender Equality


Gender Equality is important to me. As a woman, there are a lot of challenges we face to reach the same level as men in our society. There are a lot of privileges men take for granted. This cause is important to me because I want to have the same opportunities as everyone else and also don't want assumptions to be made solely because of my gender. Key social and economic issues include safety, the wage gap, equal access to education, and equal representation in media.
